How to use this calculator

The inputs are pre-populated with industry benchmarks — they're a starting framework, not your numbers yet. Review each assumption together and update them to reflect your actual costs and contracts. When the inputs are accurate, the output becomes a business case you can take internally.

Key assumptions to validate
  • Unified commerce upside Benchmarked from Shopify merchant data — adjust if your customer behaviour differs
  • Current POS costs Use your actual contract value, not list price
  • Payment processing rates Enter your blended rate across all tender types
  • Implementation costs Confirm with your SI or internal IT team
  • Hardware Adjust based on existing infrastructure and planned rollout
Results are indicative. Final savings depend on your contracts, usage patterns, and how Shopify POS is implemented.
